Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken fried rice is a flavorful and satisfying dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with tender pieces of chicken, scrambled eggs, and a mix of vegetables.

This chicken fried rice recipe is very easy to make and tastes better than takeout! It's basically cooked chicken, rice, soy sauce, and shredded egg stir-fried together. For best results, use leftover cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat
  4. Add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes
  5. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside
  6. Heat oil in the same skillet
  7. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes
  8. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper
  9. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
